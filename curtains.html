Frequently Asked Questions

We Answer Your Questions

What types of curtains does Nestora offer?

+

Nestora offers a wide selection of curtains, including blackout curtains, sheer curtains, eyelet curtains, pleated drapes, motorized curtains, and layered combinations. Our range suits both residential and commercial interiors, blending elegance with practicality.

Can I get custom-made curtains for my space?

+

Absolutely. Nestora specializes in custom-made curtains tailored to your window sizes, preferred fabric, design style, and color palette. Our team will assist you in choosing the right options to match your interior decor and functional needs.

What materials are used for Nestora curtains?

+

We use premium materials such as velvet, linen, silk, cotton, polyester, and blackout fabrics. Each fabric is carefully selected for its quality, durability, and ability to control light, privacy, and temperature.

Which curtain is best for bedrooms?

+

For bedrooms, we recommend blackout curtains or thick drapes to block sunlight and ensure privacy. Soft textures like velvet or layered curtains with sheers underneath also add a luxurious and cozy feel to your personal space.

How do curtains enhance my living or office space?

+

Curtains enhance your space by providing privacy, controlling light, reducing noise, and adding a decorative element. In commercial spaces, they contribute to a polished and professional atmosphere, while in homes, they bring warmth and style to any room.

Do you offer motorized or smart curtain options?

+

Yes, we provide motorized curtain systems for a modern and convenient lifestyle. These smart solutions can be operated via remote control or integrated with home automation systems for effortless control.
