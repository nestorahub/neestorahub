Frequently Asked Questions

We Answer Your Questions

What types of furniture does Nestora offer?

+

Nestora offers a wide range of custom and ready-made furniture, including sofas, chairs, beds, dining sets, wardrobes, TV units, and office furniture. Our pieces are designed to combine style, comfort, and functionality for every space in your home or commercial setting.

Do you provide customized furniture?

+

Yes, Nestora specializes in customized furniture. You can choose your preferred design, size, material, fabric, and color to perfectly match your interior style and space requirements. Our team works closely with you to bring your vision to life.

What materials are used in Nestora’s furniture?

+

We use premium materials like solid wood, engineered wood, MDF, stainless steel, and high-quality upholstery fabrics such as velvet, leatherette, and linen. All materials are selected for their durability, comfort, and aesthetic appeal.

Which furniture is best for my living room?

+

For your living room, consider a comfortable and stylish sofa set, a functional coffee table, side tables, and a TV unit. Choose designs that reflect your personality and color schemes that complement the overall interior. Soft fabrics and plush cushions create a cozy, welcoming atmosphere.

Can Nestora help with interior layout and furniture arrangement?

+

Yes, we offer complete interior design consultation, including layout planning and furniture arrangement to maximize comfort and functionality. Whether it’s for a single room or an entire property, our experts ensure your space looks great and serves your lifestyle.

How can Nestora furniture enhance my commercial space?

+

Nestora’s custom-made office furniture provides both aesthetic value and ergonomic support. Our office desks, chairs, and storage units are designed to boost productivity while maintaining a modern and professional appearance, helping you make a strong impression on clients and employees.
